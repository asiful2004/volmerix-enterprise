Refund Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2026

At Volmerix Enterprise, we strive to provide exceptional service. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be granted for our digital subscription services.

1. General Refund Policy

Due to the nature of digital services and subscription accounts, refunds are generally not available after service delivery. However, we offer refunds in specific circumstances as outlined below.

Important: All refund requests must be submitted within the specified timeframes and are subject to our review and approval.

2. When Are Refunds Available?

Technical Issues

If the delivered account/service experiences technical issues that prevent normal usage within the first 24 hours of delivery, you may be eligible for a refund or replacement.

Service Unavailability

If we are unable to deliver the service within 48 hours of payment confirmation due to circumstances beyond your control, a full refund will be provided.

Platform Suspension

If the account is suspended or terminated by the platform provider (YouTube, Google, etc.) within the first 7 days due to no fault of your own, you may be eligible for a refund.

Billing Errors

If you were charged incorrectly or multiple times for the same service, we will process a full refund for the erroneous charges.

3. When Are Refunds Not Available?

Refunds will not be provided in the following circumstances:

  • User Error: Issues caused by incorrect usage, password changes, or account sharing violations
  • Platform Policies: Account suspension due to violation of YouTube/Google terms of service
  • Change of Mind: Dissatisfaction with the service after 24 hours of delivery
  • Expired Subscriptions: Requests made after the subscription period has ended
  • Third-Party Issues: Problems caused by internet connectivity, device issues, or third-party software
  • Fraudulent Requests: Attempts to obtain refunds through deceptive means

4. Refund Request Timeframes

Technical Issues

Refund requests for technical issues must be submitted within 24 hours of service delivery.

Service Unavailability

Requests due to our inability to deliver must be made within 72 hours of payment.

Platform Suspension

Claims related to platform suspension must be submitted within 7 days of the suspension.

Billing Errors

Billing error claims must be reported within 30 days of the transaction.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

1

Gather Information

Collect your order number, payment confirmation, and details of the issue.

2

Contact Support

Email us at refunds@volmerix.com with your refund request and supporting evidence.

3

Provide Evidence

Include screenshots, error messages, or other proof of the issue.

4

Wait for Review

We will review your request within 24-48 hours and respond with our decision.

6. Refund Processing

Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days from approval. The time for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and bank.

Refund Methods

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 1-3 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-7 business days
  • Cryptocurrency: 1-24 hours

Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds or service credits instead of full refunds, depending on the nature and timing of the issue.

7. Subscription-Specific Refunds

Monthly Subscriptions

For monthly subscriptions, refunds are available only if technical issues occur within the first 24 hours of the billing cycle. No refunds for subsequent months.

Yearly Subscriptions

Yearly subscriptions may be eligible for prorated refunds if technical issues occur within the first 7 days. After 7 days, refunds are not available for yearly plans.

Family Plans

Family plan refunds follow the same guidelines as individual plans. Refunds are not available if accounts are shared beyond the authorized family members.

8. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision, you may request a secondary review by contacting our management team. We are committed to fair resolution of all legitimate refund requests.

For escalated disputes, please email disputes@volmerix.com with detailed information about your case and our previous response.
